Who are we?

We are a group of scientists that believe scientists' pioneering achievements can be better delivered to people by user-friendly, customizable and extensible software.

Why are we here?

It is challenging to publish your work on a high-impact journal. It is even more challenging to make your work truly impactful on people.

Let's face it. You may understand your beautiful algorithm top to bottom, but people do not. You may be familiar with UNIX command lines and various parameters of your package, but people are not. The technology is changing everyday but your previous work rely on legacy systems that people don't use anymore.

Imagine there is a day, your idea can be easily implemented with a user-friendly interface on a cloud-based computing platform to utilize their latest CPU/GPU and parallel software programming technology. Your data, should it be tables, medical images, or real-time photos, can be easily shared with other scientists. Just a couple of clicks, your work is delivered to people, ordinary people, seamlessly.

We are here to make it happen.

What do we do?

Science gateway is web + data + science idea. It is a web platform that synthesizes data sources, data analysis models and visualization methods. Science gateway simplifies the input and output of complex data analysis via the web and utilizes high-performance computation facilities to provide interactive experiences.

Ultimately, science gateway enables ordinary people to participate in otherwise complicated and even boring scientific activities.

We design science gateway software for you to open the gate of your scientific world to people.
