Who are we?

We are a group of scientists that all beleive scientists' pioneering achievements can be better delivered to people by user-friendly, customizable and extensible software .

Why are we here?

It is challenging to publish your work on a hig-impact journal. It even more challenging to make you work truly impactful on people.

Let's face it. You may understand your beautiful algorithm top to bottom, but people do not. You may be familiar with UNIX command lines and various parameters of your package, but peope are not. The technology is changing everyday but your previous work rely on legacy systems that people don't use anymore.

Imagine there is a day, your idea can be easily implemented with a user-friendly interface on a cloud-based computing platform to utilize their latest CPU/GPU and parallel software programming technology. Your data, should it be tables, medical images, or real-time photos, can be easily shared with other scicentists. Just couple of clicks, your work will be delivered to people, ordinary people, seamlessly.

We are here to make that day happen.

Science Gateway

In our understanding, Gateway is Web + Data Computing . It is a web platform that synthesizes data sources, data analysis models and visuliztion methods and opens the gate to the scientific world to people.

A science gateway simplifies the input and output of complex data analysis via the web and utilizes high-performance computaion facilities to provide interactive experiences.

As a result, the science gateways enable ordinary people to participate otherwise complicated and even boring scientific activities.
